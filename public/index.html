The Process

How PharmaRevo Works

A simple 4-step process to ensure medicines reach those who need them most.

1

Register & Donate

Individuals, pharmacies, or companies register and submit surplus medicines.

2

Verification

Our admin team verifies medicine quality, expiry, and safety standards.

3

Redistribution

Approved medicines are dispatched to registered NGOs and hospitals.

4

Safe Disposal

Expired medicines are routed to authorized disposal channels safely.

Our Mission

Why PharmaRevo Exists

Every year, millions of medicines are wasted while millions of people cannot afford basic healthcare. PharmaRevo bridges this gap by creating a transparent, technology-driven ecosystem for medicine redistribution.

We work with individuals, pharmacies, pharmaceutical companies, and NGOs to ensure that no medicine goes to waste — and no patient goes without.
